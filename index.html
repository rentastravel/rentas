My little blog




  • Devrim Stadium

    Middle East Technical University was the flag bearer of anti-imperialism, anti-capitalism, resistance and revolutionism in Turkey. In 1968, "DEVRİM" which means revolution, was written on METU stadium in giant letters that no one dared to erase. Since then, it is called as Devrim Stadyumu/Revolution Stadium.


    Scientia Dux Vitae Certissimus

    Scientia Dux Vitae Certissimus, Science is the truest guide in life, is the motto for METU. Atatürk always pressed the importance of science in building a great nation. As Atatürk once said, “If one day, my words are against science, choose science.”


    Atatürk

    Mustafa Kemal Atatürk was a revolutionary statesman and the founding father of the Republic of Turkey. He undertook sweeping progressive reforms, which modernized Turkey into a secular, industrial nation.


  • Petronas Twin Towers

    The Petronas Twin Towers are twin skyscrapers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. They were tallest buildings in the world from 1998 to 2004 and remain the tallest twin towers in the world.


    Istanbul

    Istanbul is a major city in Turkey. It is located on both Asia and Europe. It is one of the best city to visit in Turkey especially for its arts, architectures and history.


    Baklava

    Baklava is a Turkish traditional sweet pastry. It is a must try food in Turkey. It was one of the most popular sweet pastries of Ottoman cuisine.
